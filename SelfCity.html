The Self and the City

This project is a portrait of the city - Düsseldorf - seen one person at a time.

What does a place become when we see it not through its skyline, but through its people?
The SELF points to the deeply personal: identity, emotion, presence.
The CITY gestures outward: movement, society, the crowd.

It is an illusion that photos are made with the camera… they are made with the eye, heart, and head.

Henri Cartier-Bresson
